The Sustainable Business Model

This isn't just a social project; it's a complete, sustainable, and scalable business. Our primary customer is the government, creating a reliable B2G (Business-to-Government) revenue stream.

Our Three Revenue Streams

1. PRIMARY: B2G Subscriptions

Government departments pay a fixed monthly or annual fee (e.g., **‚Çπ1,00,000/month**) for continuous, secure access to the data API. This provides a predictable, recurring revenue stream.

2. SECONDARY: Usage-Based

For smaller agencies or specific projects, we offer a "pay-per-call" model (e.g., **‚Çπ0.50 per query**). This scales directly with data consumption and provides a low barrier to entry.

3. TERTIARY: NGO Premium

A small, optional subscription for large-scale NGOs who want advanced features like custom branding, priority support, or deeper analytics dashboards.
